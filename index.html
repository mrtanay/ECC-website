Welcome to Engineering Consulting Collaborative

"At Engineering - Consulting Collaborative we collaborate the efforts of engineering and business students to host/participate various competitions which will foster the development of problem-solving, and cross-discipline teamwork essential for working in today’s corporate environment. The club will be working with Penn State’s School of Engineering Design, Technology, and Professional Programs (SEDTAPP) that delivers effective engineering education through active, collaborative, project-based, and professionally oriented classroom experiences."
